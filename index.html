Everything you need to know about Naviget

What is Naviget? +
Naviget is an innovative ride-hailing platform that simplifies your commuting experience by providing seamless access to multiple ride options, including both ONDC and non-ONDC partners, all in one place.
How does Naviget work? +
Naviget aggregates ride options from various providers, allowing you to compare prices and book the most suitable ride for your needs through a single, user-friendly interface.
What makes Naviget different from other ride-hailing apps? +
Naviget uniquely combines both ONDC and non-ONDC partners, offering you more choices and better prices while maintaining a seamless booking experience.
How does Naviget make money? +
Naviget earns through small service fees on successful bookings while maintaining competitive prices for users.
How do I book a ride on Naviget? +
Simply open the app, enter your destination, compare available options, and book your preferred ride with just a few taps.
